## Corrected the Vulkan infinity patch for GLSL

- **WHAT:** Replaced the invalid global `const float` bit-cast in `topk_moe.comp` with a `NEGATIVE_INFINITY` macro evaluated at six function-local use sites, while retaining exact bit-casts for the two already-local shader initializers.
- **WHY:** GLSL rejects `uintBitsToFloat` in a global constant initializer even though the expression is valid at runtime.
- **HOW:** Upgraded the patch identity to `vulkan-infinity-v2`, made the patcher require the pinned one-plus-six occurrence shape, aligned Bash/PowerShell/Rust gates, and updated deterministic fixtures and rejection tests.
- **IMPACT:** The patch preserves exact IEEE negative infinity without undefined division or an illegal global initializer, unblocking Linux and Windows shader generation for the next exact artifact run.
