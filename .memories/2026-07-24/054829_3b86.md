## WHAT

Closed the final macOS x64 packaging test findings.

## WHY

The manual release workflow has two conditional matrix branches. Profile counts plus any Intel runner did not prove both x64 entries were paired with `macos-15-intel`. The CI lane helper also needed a safe future top-level-job boundary.

## HOW

- Count the exact inline x64/Intel/Metal/Bash release object and require two occurrences, one per conditional matrix branch.
- Mutate one branch to `macos-15`; the old total profile count remains three while the structural pair count drops to one.
- Stop artifact-validation lane extraction at either the next lane or the next exact two-space top-level job.
- Add a synthetic future-job test for the extractor boundary.

Release matrix branch drift and future CI job growth now fail locally instead of preserving misleading substring counts.
